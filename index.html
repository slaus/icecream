Our story

The goal of our project is to create an exquisite ice cream brand that goes beyond the ordinary. We strive for our customers to receive not only satisfaction, but also true pleasure, which is hidden under each wrapper.

"Iceborn" ice cream will be an incomparable source of pleasure for adults, a gentle fairy-tale holiday for children and a magical memory of youth for grandparents. Our main goal is that every person receives unique moments of happiness and experiences unforgettable emotions of joy thanks to Iceborn ice cream.

Hello, everyone!

My name is Albina, and I am the proud CEO of an amazing ice cream brand called "Iceborn." Even though I'm only 7 years old, I believe that age doesn't matter when it comes to making a difference in the world.

Iceborn is not just any ordinary ice cream brand. We have a special mission to make the world a better place, one scoop at a time. How do we do that, you ask? Well, it's quite simple. We believe that people's happiness is key to creating a better world. And what better way to bring joy and happiness than through delicious and healthy ice cream?

By sharing the happiness of Iceborn ice cream, I hope to inspire others to find joy in the simple pleasures of life. Together, we can make the world a better place, one smile at a time. So, join me on this sweet journey, and let's indulge in the happiness that Iceborn brings to each and every one of us.

Remember, life is sweeter when we're happy, and Iceborn is here to make sure of it!
