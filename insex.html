Our story

The goal of our project is to create an exquisite ice cream brand that goes beyond the ordinary. We strive for our customers to receive not only satisfaction, but also true pleasure, which is hidden under each wrapper.

"Iceborn" ice cream will be an incomparable source of pleasure for adults, a gentle fairy-tale holiday for children and a magical memory of youth for grandparents. Our main goal is that every person receives unique moments of happiness and experiences unforgettable emotions of joy thanks to Iceborn ice cream.
